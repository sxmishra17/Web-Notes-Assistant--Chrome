# Privacy Policy for Web Notes Assistant

**Effective Date:** April 5, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use the Web Notes Assistant Chrome extension ("Extension"), created by YuvaTech. This privacy policy is meant to help you understand how we handle your data when you use our Extension.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Web Notes Assistant is designed with your privacy in mind.** 
The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data. 

All data generated by the Extension, including:
- Highlighted text
- Captured screenshots
- Notes and Workspaces metadata

is processed locally and stored exclusively on your device utilizing Chrome's local storage APIs.

## 2. Permissions Required

To function properly, the Extension requests the following permissions:
- **`activeTab`**: To enable highlighting and screenshot capture on the active page you are viewing.
- **`storage`**: To save your notes, workspaces, and preferences locally on your device.
- **`tabs` & `scripting`**: To inject the necessary content scripts and components (like the sidebar) into web pages so you can take notes seamlessly across tabs.
- **`host_permissions`** (`<all_urls>`): To allow the Extension to overlay its functionality (sidebar and highlights) on any webpage you visit.

None of these permissions are used to track your browsing history or transmit data to external servers.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Because the Extension does not collect or store any of your data on our servers, there is no data to share. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information or any browsing data.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
